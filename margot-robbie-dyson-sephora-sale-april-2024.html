What is the Dyson Airwrap?

The TikTok-viral hair tool dries, straightens, and creates voluminous waves and curls. It’s designed with all hair types in mind, ranging from coily and curly to wavy and straight.

How Margot Robbie Used the Dyson Airwrap

For the 2023 Met Gala, Robbie’s hairstylist, Bryce Scarlett, used the round volumizing brush attachment on the Dyson Airwrap to give her hair supermodel bombshell volume. After creating bouncy curls with the 1.6-inch Dyson barrel, he set each section with a Velcro roller and removed the rollers after they cooled. He sprayed and backcombed each section, used a boar bristle flat brush, and smoothed the ends with the Dyson Airwrap’s Coanda smoothing attachment.

What Our Team Thinks
